Is Juicy Burger Month the same as Juicy Burger Cup? No, these events are not the same. Juicy Burger Cup is a contest in which you and others like you vote to decide who makes Beijing’s best burger out of a pool of over 100 burger joints. Alongside these time-tested and tasty selections is an amazing array of 17 new burger places – more than a quarter of the 64-contestant bracket – that were not in our Top 100 last year. These include new standouts such as COMMUNE·X, Ye Brewing and Apollo.
